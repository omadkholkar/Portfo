About me

I’m Omkar Arun Madkholkar, a passionate data enthusiast based in Mulund, Mumbai. My journey into the world of data science is quite unique, having transitioned from a successful seven-year career as an Art Director in the professional hairdressing industry. While my creative background provided a solid foundation in artistry and client management, I found myself increasingly drawn to the analytical and problem-solving aspects of data science.

I enrolled in a Post Graduate Program in Data Science and Business Analytics at the Aegis School of Business, Data Science & Telecommunication in Mumbai. This rigorous program equipped me with essential skills in Python, machine learning, statistics, Tableau, and deep learning. The academic environment allowed me to delve deeply into the theoretical aspects of data science while also providing opportunities for practical application.

One of the most rewarding experiences during my studies was my internship at Medius.ai. Here, I had the opportunity to apply my learning in real-world scenarios. I performed detailed analyses of loan portfolios, examined borrower behaviors, and generated comprehensive reports for various clients. This role not only enhanced my technical skills but also taught me the importance of data security and client coordination.
